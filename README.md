## 📌 Overview

**Child Screen Detector** is an AI-powered digital safety system designed to protect children from harmful, misleading, and inappropriate online content.

With the rapid growth of digital platforms, children are increasingly exposed to unsafe content, misinformation, addictive content loops, and online risks. This project uses **Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, OCR, and Content Analysis** to analyze screen activity and identify potential threats in real time.

The goal is to create a safer digital environment where children can explore online content while parents receive awareness and safety insights.

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

Children today spend significant time on digital platforms, but they may unknowingly encounter:

- ❌ Misleading information and fake content
- ❌ Harmful videos and images
- ❌ Unsafe keywords and online risks
- ❌ Excessive screen usage patterns
- ❌ Addictive content recommendation loops

Traditional parental controls only block websites or applications but do not understand the actual content being viewed.

---

# 💡 Solution

Child Screen Detector introduces an intelligent AI monitoring system that:

- Captures and analyzes screen content
- Extracts text using OCR technology
- Detects unsafe or suspicious content
- Provides real-time safety alerts
- Helps parents understand children's digital behavior

---

# ✨ Features

## 🧠 AI Content Analysis
- Detects potentially harmful digital content
- Identifies misleading or unsafe information

## 👁️ Screen Monitoring
- Real-time screen activity analysis
- Tracks content displayed on the device

## 🔍 OCR Text Detection
- Extracts text from images and videos
- Analyzes keywords and context

## 🚨 Safety Alerts
- Generates warnings when unsafe content is detected
- Provides recommendations for safer browsing

## 🤖 AI Safety Assistant
- Answers child safety-related questions
- Guides users about online risks

## 📊 Digital Behavior Insights
- Helps analyze screen usage patterns
- Encourages healthy digital habits
